🖌️ Modern Design

Modern Design is a visual style that focuses on simplicity, clarity, and functionality. It often features clean lines, minimal elements, readable typography, and a balance between aesthetics and usability.

In web design, modern design principles help create a better user experience by removing clutter and emphasizing what matters most. Less is more!

  • 🎯 Clean Layout
  • 🌈 Harmonious Colors
  • 📐 Grid-based Structure
  • 💡 Focus on Usability

Modern Design isn't just about looking good — it's about **functionality with beauty**. ✨
